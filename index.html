Nairobi, Kenya

Kenya sits along the equator in East Africa, bordered by the Indian Ocean to the southeast. Nairobi, the capital, is known as a growing hub for technology and innovation in the region, often nicknamed the "Silicon Savannah." The country is also famous for its wildlife, the Great Rift Valley, and its long-distance running tradition.
